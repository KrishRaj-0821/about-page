Our Success Stories

Software Services & Solutions, SSS is a rapidly growing Software Services and Solutions (SSS), a unit of S S Systems Pvt Ltd, was established in 2017 with a mission to provide innovative technology solutions and services. Since its inception, SSS has achieved numerous milestones that have positioned it as a significant player in the technology industry, particularly in India.

One of the earliest achievements in SSS’s journey was receiving the Times Excellence Award in 2021. This prestigious award recognized the company’s outstanding contributions to the technology sector, highlighting its excellence in digital marketing, software development, IT consulting, and more. The award underscored SSS’s commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, helping to solidify its reputation as a leader in the industry.

In August 2022, SSS was featured in FORBES' special edition "India @ 75", a publication that commemorated India’s 75th year of independence by celebrating companies and individuals making significant contributions to the nation’s progress. Being featured in such a respected global publication was a significant honor for SSS. It highlighted the company's impact on the technology sector in India and beyond, showcasing its innovative approach and role in shaping the future of technology in the country.

The momentum continued into February 2023, when SSS reached another major milestone. The company was invited by the Bihar Industries Association and the IIT Patna Incubation Centre to share its journey on the TEDx Platform. This invitation was a testament to the innovative spirit and entrepreneurial drive that characterize SSS. On the TEDx stage, Dr. Aamir Junaid Ahmad, the CEO of SSS, shared the company’s success story with a diverse audience that included budding entrepreneurs, IIT students, and professors. His presentation captivated the audience, demonstrating how a company based in Patna, Bihar, could make a significant mark on the global tech landscape. The attendees were highly impressed by SSS’s journey, which inspired many aspiring technologists and business leaders to pursue their entrepreneurial dreams.

Another significant achievement for SSS was its collaboration with the Indian Railways' Sonpur Division. This project involved the implementation of advanced software solutions to enhance the operational efficiency of the railway division. Leveraging its expertise in technology and innovation, SSS helped streamline various processes within the Sonpur Division, contributing to the modernization of the Indian Railways. This project not only showcased SSS's technical capabilities but also its ability to handle large-scale, complex projects, further establishing its position as a reliable technology partner.

In addition to its accomplishments under SSS, the company has also made significant strides with its startup InternshipWALA. Launched with the aim of bridging the gap between education and industry, InternshipWALA has quickly become a popular platform for students seeking internships. In 2021, it was awarded the Best Online Platform for Internships by Business Mint, recognizing its impact on the educational sector. The platform has been instrumental in providing practical experience to students, preparing them for successful careers in various fields. The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) also acknowledged InternshipWALA’s efforts, sending a letter of appreciation for the platform’s significant role in benefiting millions of students across India. Through InternshipWALA, students gain valuable hands-on experience, enhancing their professional development and readiness for the workforce.

Since its establishment, SSS has continually expanded its range of services, offering solutions in areas such as digital marketing, software development, IT consulting, and more. The company’s growth can be attributed to its focus on innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. SSS prides itself on delivering customized solutions that meet the unique needs of its clients, whether they are small startups or large enterprises.

Today, Software Services and Solutions stands as a testament to what can be achieved with dedication, innovation, and a commitment to excellence. From its roots in Patna to its recognition on national and international platforms, SSS continues to push the boundaries of technology, driving forward with a mission to empower businesses through innovative solutions. Each new project and achievement solidifies SSS's position as a leader in the technology industry, committed to making a positive impact in the world of software and services.
